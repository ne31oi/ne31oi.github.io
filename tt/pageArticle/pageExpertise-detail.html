Travel technologies

ThinkTravelTech TTT is a not-for-profit and non-governmental organisation with the headquarters in London and in Montecarlo. Our mission is to analyse and promote the understanding of major innovations influencing the modern tourism industry. Being the member of the Travellers club London Pall Mall, TTT shares the values of open travel as a mean of true “peoples diplomacy”.

Established in 2018 by international businessman Mikhail Ignatiev who has over 30 years of success in the travel industry and related projects, TTT is a dynamically growing internet web information and streaming service helping to popularise and develop the best innovations in the industry.
